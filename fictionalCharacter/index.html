Ethan Winters



About Ethan...


Ethan Winters was the protagonist behind Resident Evil 7: Biohazard as well as Resident Evil 8: Village, and is most well known for being a very bland character. He is a goofball, a scoundrel, and a loving yet misguided daddy-man. Playing these games show just how absolutely hilarious it is to watch this 80's sitcom of a protagonist stumble through absolute hell, and just go "Well shucks, thats not very good!" He is a blank character shoe-horned into an already pre-established franchise, but honestly his goofy one-liners make most just snort a bit before remebering that he isn't believable at all.
