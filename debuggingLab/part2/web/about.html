St. Lucia is an island in the Caribbean Sea. It is estimated to have a population of 160,000 with the capital city being Castries. The French first settled the island in 1660 when they signed a treaty with the locals. The British then fought them for control, which they secured in 1814. However, in 1979 Lucia gained their full independence back.

Now there are many things to do in St. Lucia like hiking, deep-sea fishing, snorkeling, boating, adventuring or just spending time with the one you love in the sun!
