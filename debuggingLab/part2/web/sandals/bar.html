There are three Sandals resorts on the island of St. Lucia, which include Regency, Halcyon and Grande. The Sandals resorts have many amenities and luxuries such as the beautiful pools and swim up bars. If you are tired of salt water in your eyes and mouth you can cool off on one of the many pools at each resort. For example, at the Regency Golf Resort and Spa, where I stayed, there are three public pools not to mention all the private pools available with suites.

Now there are many things to do in St. Lucia like hiking, deep-sea fishing, snorkeling, boating, adventuring or just spending time with the one you love in the sun!
